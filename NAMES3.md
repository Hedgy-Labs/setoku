# Name search, round 3 — 50 candidates, pronounceable edition (2026-06-10)

> Brief after round-2 feedback: **easy to pronounce** (an American colleague says it right cold), explore **droid-designation names** (R2-D2; Cloudflare R2/D1; AWS S3), and prefer **plain math words** — empty set, normal, basis — over scholarly Latin.

**Method:** ~900 fresh candidates this round (plain-math compounds, accountant verification idioms, droid word+digit grids, counting idioms), all whois-verified against Verisign on 2026-06-10 (~250 came back available — the plain-math and word+digit veins are far more open than dictionary words). Every name below passes the say-it-cold test. Nothing is reserved by a check — register the winner immediately.
