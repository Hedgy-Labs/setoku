## Recommendation (Claude's pick): **Setoku**

1. **Its meaning _is_ the product.** "The innermost set" — the deep layer that makes your AI intelligent about the business — is the one-liner. Setminus has the best _sound_, but its semantics point the wrong way: `A \ B` is about _removing_ things; this product's story is about _adding_ grounded knowledge. Cool sticker, wrong thesis.
2. **It synthesizes the whole search.** The construction is ours (brainoku), with both morphemes upgraded: "brain" → "set" (consumer → math), "oku" kept. The origin story — "coined from set theory and the Japanese word for innermost" — is a pitch in itself.
3. **It's the most ownable.** Setminus/Sumfold are dictionary-adjacent (harder trademark, "clever engineer name" ceiling). Setoku is a true coined mark — clean Google results, easy trademark, room to grow (Sonos/Hulu pattern).

**Flip case for Setminus:** if go-to-market were dev-tools (selling to engineers), the `\setminus` wink does real work. For heads of data/ops at enterprises, it doesn't — and Setoku reads premium to both audiences.

**Action:** register setoku.com today; grab setminus.com too (~$10, runner-up insurance).
